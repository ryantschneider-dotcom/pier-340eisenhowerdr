Tight Vacancy, Rising Demand

Savannah's office market entered 2026 with a 7.8% vacancy rate — well below the national average of 13.5% (CBRE, Q1 2026). The Southside submarket, anchored by the medical corridor and government offices, has historically outperformed the broader market due to stable institutional demand.

Southside Submarket Strength

The Eisenhower Drive / Waters Avenue corridor is Savannah's primary professional services hub, hosting medical practices, law firms, financial advisors, and government contractors. Proximity to St. Joseph's/Candler Health System and Memorial Health drives consistent demand for professional office space in the 1,000–3,000 SF range.

Below-Market Rate Opportunity

At $28.00/SF/yr Modified Gross, this listing is priced 20.6% below the Savannah market average asking rent of $35.26/SF/yr. For a 1,250 SF suite, that translates to approximately $9,075/year in savings versus a comparable market-rate space — a meaningful advantage for a growing practice or professional services firm.
